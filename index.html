How It Works

Don Golf Tags are custom bag tags with unique QR codes. When scanned, each tag can be claimed through a short form, but only by one player at a time.

Once claimed, your name and location appear on the public leaderboard. You can only hold one tag at a time, if someone else scans and claims your tag, it transfers to them.

The goal? Climb the ranks. Each tag has a number, and lower numbers are better. Tag #001 is the ultimate prize. To rise, challenge someone with a better tag, if you beat them, you take theirs and they drop down to whichever rank you held.
